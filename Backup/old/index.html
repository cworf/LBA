Welcome to Montana Screen Printing, home of Custom Screen Printing & Embroidery. Our high quality, customized products are used for individual gifts and gift programs, sport uniforms or fan wear, corporate casual wear, staff apparel, advertising, special events, fundraisers, tournaments, family reunions, and so on. A sample of these products include: T-shirts, sweatshirts, polos, golf shirts, denim and dress shirts, workwear, sweats, jackets, windbreakers, sweaters, team wear, shorts, aprons, tote bags, towels, and many more.

Our family-run business emphasizes quality and customer satisfaction, at affordable prices. All products are customizable to your needs and specifications. Located in Missoula, MT, our business hours are Monday through Friday, 9 am to 5 pm and Saturday, 9 am to noon.
